# Admin AI Demo Script

## Hook

"Most businesses run on disconnected tools. Admin AI is the last-mile integration that turns your Gmail, Calendar, and LinkedIn activity into one actionable relationship dashboard."

## Demo Flow

1. Open Dashboard and explain engagement and follow-up priority metrics.
2. Show Priority Contacts and identify who needs action today.
3. Open Contacts and walk through a contact record with objectives, tags, and sentiment.
4. Add a new interaction to show timeline updates.
5. Go to Integrations and connect Gmail/LinkedIn/Calendar in local mode.
6. Run Sync All and show imported follow-up opportunities.
7. Open AI Messages and generate three follow-up options.
8. Show Analytics and weekly report for leadership snapshot.
9. End on Settings and explain local-first privacy model.

## Closing

"Admin AI removes manual follow-up chaos and gives your team one place to run relationship workflows."
