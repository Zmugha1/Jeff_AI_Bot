The Framework

Where are you operating right now?

The Skill Threshold Zone is the operating band where human expertise and AI capability meet at exactly the right ratio.

Below the Zone

You are doing work AI should be doing. Reformatting notes. Summarizing documents. Searching for information that already exists. Expert time burned on execution work.

Inside the Zone
This is where you belong

You are doing only what only you can do. Making the call no algorithm can make. Catching the signal no document contains. Everything else is handled.

Above the Zone

You have handed too much to AI. You have stopped interrogating the outputs. Your expertise is leaking out. This is more dangerous than being below the Zone because it feels productive.
